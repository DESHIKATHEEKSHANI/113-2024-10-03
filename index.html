Titanic is a 1997 American epic romantic disaster film directed, written, co-produced and co-edited by James Cameron. Incorporating both historical and fictionalized aspects, it is based on accounts of the sinking of RMS Titanic in 1912.

It tells the fictionalized love story of Jack Dawson and Rose DeWitt Bukater , two passengers from different social classes who meet aboard the RMS Titanic during its ill-fated maiden voyage in 1912. The film blends historical events, including the ship's sinking, with a poignant narrative about love and sacrifice.
Featuring iconic performances from Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet, "Titanic" became a cultural phenomenon, known for its groundbreaking visual effects and memorable soundtrack. It won 11 Academy Awards, including Best Picture.

Cast
  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Jack Dawson

    • A poor artist who wins a ticket to board the Titanic and falls in love with Rose.



  • Kate Winslet as Rose DeWitt Bukater

    • A young, upper-class woman engaged to a wealthy but controlling fiancé; she seeks to escape her privileged life and finds love with Jack.

  • Billy Zane as Cal Hockley

    • Rose's wealthy fiancé, who represents the societal expectations of the time and serves as the antagonist to her love for Jack.



  • Bill Paxton as Brock Lovett

    • A treasure hunter searching for a priceless necklace in the wreck of the Titanic; he frames the modern-day storyline.

  • David Warner as Spicer Lovejoy

    • Cal’s bodyguard and confidant, who is loyal to his wealthy employer and suspicious of Jack.



  • Kathy Bates as Molly Brown

    • A real-life Titanic survivor nicknamed "The Unsinkable Molly Brown," who befriends Jack and Rose and helps them navigate the class differences.

These characters drive the film's emotional arc and highlight the themes of love, class struggle, and tragedy.

Reviews

Titanic, launched on May 31, 1911, and set sail on its maiden voyage from Southampton on April 10, 1912, with 2,240 passengers and crew on board. On April 15, 1912, after striking an iceberg, Titanic broke apart and sank to the bottom of the ocean, taking with it the lives of more than 1,500 passengers and crew.
